## Context

Research project at the **Laboratory of Bioresources – McGill University**, focused on building an affordable and open-source robotic platform for precision agriculture research.

## System

A gantry robot equipped with cameras and sensors autonomously navigates over plant beds to collect high-resolution spatiotemporal data. The goal is to model plant growth patterns over time with minimal human intervention.

Key aspects:
- Mechanical and electronic design from scratch
- Camera and sensor integration for multi-modal data collection
- Control system for autonomous navigation
