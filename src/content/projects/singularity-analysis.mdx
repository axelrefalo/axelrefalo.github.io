## Context

This work is my Master's thesis at the Control and Robotics Laboratory (CoRo) at ÉTS, under the supervision of Prof. Ilian Bonev and Prof. Clément Gosselin.

## Problem

Six-revolute-joint serial manipulators — the most common architecture in industrial robotics — exhibit singular configurations where the robot loses degrees of freedom or becomes uncontrollable. Understanding, classifying and avoiding these singularities is critical for safe and efficient path planning.

## Contributions

- Comparative analysis of singularities, manipulability and cuspidality across collaborative robots from **Universal Robot, Doosan, ABB and Fanuc**
- Implementation of an **Inverse Kinematics solver** for a general 6R serial manipulator, interfacing with RoboDK (C++)
- Focused study on ABB's GoFa 5 robot arm, leading to a publication at ICRA 2026

## Publication

Refalo, A., Bonev, I., and Gosselin, C. *"Singularity Analysis of ABB's GoFa 5 Robot Arm,"* accepted at the **IEEE International Conference on Robotics and Automation (ICRA) 2026**, Vienna.
